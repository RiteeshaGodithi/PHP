Arizona established the &quot;right to remain silent&quot; and other rights for suspects. Professor Bruce Peabody talks about the myths and realities of the Miranda warning.</p> <h3><a href="http://view2.fdu.edu/fdu-whats-new/press-releases/2015-2016/05/braverman-named-petrocelli-dean/">Braverman to lead Petrocelli</a></h3> <p>Lisa Braverman has been named dean of Petrocelli College of Continuing Studies, bringing with her extensive experience in adult learning and continuing education.</p> <h3><a href="http://view2.fdu.edu/fdu-whats-new/press-releases/2015-2016/05/gillian-small-appointed-university-provost-and-senior-vice-president-for-academic-affairs/">Gillian Small named University Provost</a></h3> <p>Dr. Small, currently vice chancellor for research at CUNY, starts on Aug. 1. She will also serve as senior vice president for academic affairs.</p> <p><a href="http://view2.fdu.edu/fdu-whats-new/"><cite>ALL NEWS AND SPOTLIGHTS</cite></a></p>

                    <section class="category">

He taught cross-cultural perspectives and creative writing, and was known to use binoculars in class and carry a flyswatter in the halls.</p> <h3><a href="http://www.capemaycountyherald.com/community/business/article_a454b420-240d-11e6-bdcb-b3744e9ae59a.html">Atlantic Cape partnership</a></h3> <p>Atlantic Cape Community College and FDU have signed a scholars agreement, providing dual admission and financial incentives to students who study first at Atlantic Cape.</p> <h3><a href="http://view2.fdu.edu/fdu-whats-new/feature-stories/2015-2016/05/graduation-2016-social-media-recap/">Graduation 2016 Storify</a></h3> <p>Relive Commencement through this collection of photos, tweets and videos from the FDU community!</p> <p><a href="http://inside.fdu.edu/features/index.html"><cite>ALL FEATURES AND PROFILES</cite></a></p>
